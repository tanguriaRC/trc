About Tanguria Recreation Club

Tanguria Recreation Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to enriching our community through cultural events, charitable activities, and social gatherings. Founded in 2010, we have been bringing people together to celebrate our shared heritage and values.

Our mission is to preserve cultural traditions while supporting community development through education, arts, and social welfare programs.
