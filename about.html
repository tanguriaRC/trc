About Us

Discover our history, mission, and the people behind Tanguria Recreation Club.

Our Organization

Tanguria Recreation Club was founded in 2010 by a group of community members who shared a passion for preserving cultural heritage and promoting community well-being. What started as small gatherings in a community hall has grown into a vibrant organization with over 500 members.

Our club serves as a hub for cultural exchange, community service, and recreational activities. We organize various events throughout the year, from traditional cultural celebrations to charity initiatives that support local schools, shelters, and healthcare facilities.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote cultural heritage while fostering community development through education, arts, and social welfare programs that enhance the quality of life for all members of our community.

Our Vision

A thriving, inclusive community where cultural traditions are celebrated, mutual support is practiced, and every individual has the opportunity to grow and contribute to the collective well-being.

Our Activities

Cultural Functions

Annual celebrations of traditional festivals, music concerts, dance performances, and art exhibitions that showcase our rich cultural heritage.

Organizing Various Festivals

We organize various festivals like Kali Puja, Rath Yatra, etc. in our community.

Education Initiatives

Scholarships, tutoring programs, and educational resources for underprivileged students to support their academic growth and development.

Community Outreach

Regular donation drives, health camps, and support services for vulnerable community members, especially during times of need.
